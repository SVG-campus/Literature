Plot Summary: (Provide a brief summary)
Scout Finch grows up in the racially charged atmosphere of Maycomb, Alabama, during the Great Depression. Her father, Atticus Finch, is a lawyer who defends a black man, Tom Robinson, falsely accused of raping a white woman, Mayella Ewell. The trial and events surrounding it expose Scout and her brother, Jem, to the complexities of morality and injustice.
